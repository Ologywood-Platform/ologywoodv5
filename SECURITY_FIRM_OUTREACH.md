## Evaluation Criteria

### Scoring Rubric (100 points total)

**Team Experience (40 points)**
- Years in business (10 points)
- Industry experience (10 points)
- Certifications and credentials (10 points)
- Client references (10 points)

**Proposed Methodology (30 points)**
- OWASP Top 10 coverage (10 points)
- Custom scope support (10 points)
- Testing approach (10 points)

**Cost & Timeline (20 points)**
- Budget alignment (10 points)
- Timeline feasibility (10 points)

**Communication & Support (10 points)**
- Responsiveness (5 points)
- Clarity of proposal (5 points)

### Scoring Guide

- **90-100:** Excellent - Highly recommended
- **80-89:** Good - Recommended
- **70-79:** Acceptable - Consider
- **Below 70:** Not recommended

## Pre-Engagement Checklist

### Before Engagement Starts

- [ ] Contract signed by both parties
- [ ] NDA executed
- [ ] Scope document finalized
- [ ] Testing environment prepared
- [ ] Test accounts created
- [ ] Access credentials provided
- [ ] API documentation shared
- [ ] System architecture documented
- [ ] Incident response team assembled
- [ ] Communication plan confirmed
- [ ] Monitoring and logging enabled
- [ ] Backup systems tested

### Day 1 of Engagement

- [ ] Kickoff meeting scheduled
- [ ] Team introductions
- [ ] Scope confirmation
- [ ] Timeline review
- [ ] Communication procedures
- [ ] Access verification
- [ ] Q&A session
- [ ] Next steps clarification

---

## Post-Engagement Checklist

### After Final Report Received

- [ ] Review findings
- [ ] Verify accuracy of findings
- [ ] Prioritize remediation
- [ ] Assign responsibilities
- [ ] Create remediation tickets
- [ ] Schedule re-testing
- [ ] Plan security improvements
- [ ] Document lessons learned
- [ ] Update security policies
- [ ] Train team on findings

---

## Success Metrics

**Successful Engagement Indicators:**

✅ Comprehensive findings covering all major attack vectors
✅ Clear, actionable remediation recommendations
✅ Professional, detailed reporting
✅ Responsive communication throughout engagement
✅ Constructive feedback and guidance
✅ Successful re-testing of fixes
✅ Strong team expertise and professionalism

❌ **Red Flags:**

❌ Incomplete or vague findings
❌ Unclear remediation recommendations
❌ Poor communication or responsiveness
❌ Unprofessional conduct
❌ Unrealistic findings or recommendations
❌ Lack of follow-up or support
